Welcome to the Orc Racing Club

O.R.C is a collection of 9,969 Orc NFTs hosted on the blockchain. Your Orc NFT is a unique digital asset and is your membership token into the Orc Racing Club , granting you access to members-only perks. These exclusive perks include 2.5% royalties of any future sales of your ORC NFT , VIP access to our future Orc Racing video game & contributing to decisions directing the future of the club.

We’re setting out a bold vision for the future of NFTs - read more in our roadmap and join us for the ride!

Fair Pricing

The price of an Orc at mint is 1 SOL. There are no price tiers and our Orcs are priced at a level to ensure ORC is as accessible as possible.

To ensure everyone gets a chance to mint an Orc, you will be limited to 1 Orc per transaction. However there is no overall limit per wallet and you can purchase multiple Orcs. O.R.C members with multiple Orcs will receive a role based on our tiered rank structure and will have larger voting rights in our holder voting system. Your Orc metadata will be permanently stored on arweave.

Our goal is to build a community - a virtual members club where like-minded collectors from all over the world can hangout and contribute to building a project on Solana.

The Specs

Each Orc is a unique and algorithmically generated collectible using base layers hand drawn by our artists.

There are over 250+ attributes across 11 categories: background, shoulder, body, clothes, necklace , markings, mouth, tusk, eyes, bling, head. These attributes determine how rare an Orc is, with each trait ranging from common to ultra rare.

Every single Orc still has membership to the Orc Racing Club and will still be able to access member-only perks, such as using your Orc in our upcoming video game and contributing to community-led decisions.

Roadmap

We have a bold vision.

This is our medium term roadmap; the O.R.C community will help direct this roadmap but also contribute to the longer term strategic vision.

  • The Launch

    The birth of our Orc-some community. 9,969 Orcs will be unleashed on the Magic Eden marketplace to mint for just 1 SOL, and this will serve as your ticket to the Orc Racing Club. There is a limit of 1 Orc per transaction, but no limit on how many you can have in your wallet.

    Once 50% of Orcs have been minted, one of our 500 early Orcs will win our $10k raffle.

  • Post Mint

    Our Orcs will be fully integrated into the garden of Magic Eden at launch, so you will immediately be able to buy and sell Orcs as you wish. But don’t be too hasty - as a gift from us, all holders will receive an exclusive airdrop post-launch! Airdrops will then happen on a quarterly basis to reward loyal Orcs. The airdrops will be separate from the original Orc collection to ensure value & exclusivity is maintained. There will never be more than 9,969 Orcs.

  • The Club

    Full website activation and the Orc Racing Clubhouse will finally be revealed. From here we will be launching our members-only merch brand “Socially Orcward” featuring collabs with upcoming NFT artists from across the space.

    Club members will get a say on merch designs and help make future O.R.C decisions via a holder voting system on our website.

    A portion of our proceeds will be donated to our chosen charity and a significant outlay will be invested back into the SOL ecosystem regularly via partnerships with new & upcoming SOL projects, and marketplaces to benefit the future of Solana NFT’s.

  • The Future

    We’ve spent a lot of time so far showing you our Orcs in all their glory. Now it’s time to talk about the “Racing” element of our club.

    We are in the early stages of developing our very own Orc Racing Club video game. Club members will have the chance to race their Orcs against any other Orc in the world on the prestigious O.R.C racetracks!

  • The Community

    The O.R.C community will help set our strategic direction beyond this point through our holder voting system.
